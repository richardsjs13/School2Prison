The Why

I started The World Is Yoursbecause there was a stretch of my journey where no one handed me a map — and I almost didn't make it up the mountain. A senior at Virginia Commonwealth University studying Information Systems, I've lived the tones a lot of us know: lack of guidance, minimal exposure to resources, being shaped by the crowd, and not recognizing where I was gifted.

Welfare. Section 8. A single-mother household. Eviction. Imposter syndrome. Being removed from classrooms. And, hardest to admit, playing a hand in my own setbacks. Everything shifted when I realized I'm the one in control of how I interpret and respond to the world around me.The value was never the destination — it was the climb. R.I.P. Kobe.If this helps even one person find their footing, the goal is achieved.

Our Platform Exists to Empower

This platform was built to provide meaningful mentorship and promote systemic equity. To maintain a safe, collaborative, and trauma-informed space, all users must uphold these core values:

  • Respect: Treat every member with dignity. We honor diverse backgrounds, cultural identities, learning differences, and personal lived experiences.
  • Constructive Growth: Mentorship is a two-way street. Feedback must be encouraging, specific, and focused on development — never punitive or demeaning.
  • Intentional Integrity: Credit creators and organizations when sharing resources. Plagiarism defeats our community's commitment to truth.
🧭

Sherpa Responsibilities

Mentors hold an inherent position of trust. To ensure a safe digital ecosystem for youth:

  • Professional Boundaries: Keep all interactions focused on education, professional, and personal growth goals.
  • Transparency: Use platform tools for announcements, goal tracking, and messaging. Do not solicit private contact information or unvetted social media accounts.
  • Mandatory Reporting: Any disclosure of self-harm, abuse, neglect, or danger must be escalated to platform administrators or appropriate support services.
🔥

Mentee Engagement & Community

You are the reason this platform exists. To get the most from your experience:

  • Active Engagement: Respond to your Sherpa's announcements, acknowledge updates, and take ownership of your goals.
  • Safe Self-Expression: Your profile should reflect your authentic self while remaining respectful and appropriate.
  • Constructive Discourse: Disagreements on sensitive topics are natural. Attacks, harassment, or hate speech will result in immediate removal.
⚖️

Zero-Tolerance & Restorative Justice

We aim to counter punitive frameworks — but user safety comes first.

  • Prohibited: Cyberbullying, targeted harassment, explicit media, hate speech, or intentional spread of harmful misinformation.
  • Our Approach: Minor infractions are met with warnings, mediated conversations, and behavior agreements — not automatic permanent exclusion.
  • The Exception: Severe infractions that compromise user safety or violate child protection laws result in permanent account termination without appeal.

By accepting, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct and help us build a community where everyone can climb their mountain.
